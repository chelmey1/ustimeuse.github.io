The first visualization shows the movement and concentration of people from one activity to another throughout a 24-hour day. The bar chart updates the concentration of people (in percentage) currently spending time in each category of time use. The ranking chart shows ranking of activities in descending order with highest to lowest concentrations of people. The rectangle area chart shows the corresponding magnitudes of concentration of people within each activity for easy and more vivid comparisons of the current concentrations of people in each time category.
